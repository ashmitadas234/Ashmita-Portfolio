Who am I?

English major turned MBA aspirant.

I'm an English major turned MBA aspirant from Kolkata that lives her life with creativity and freedom. Since childhood, I've been very into voice acting and undertaken a few passion projects of the same. I love working with people and thrive in social settings and contexts.

Versatility is what I'm good at, and I'm excited to work on varied projects of many kinds as they provide for valuable learning opportunities. Excited to hear from you!

How did I get here?

A path through words and stages.

I graduated high school in 2022 from Garden High School, and went on to pursue a three-year English honours degree from The Bhawanipur Education Society College the same year in my hometown.

I am currently pursuing an MBA from the Xaviers' Institute of Management, and am based in Bhubaneswar.

What made me?

Voices, stages, and quiet pages.

Since childhood, I've been passionate about voice acting and have undertaken a few personal projects to voice my favourite characters from anime and animated films. I am an aspiring actor, and have starred in four theatrical productions during my undergraduate years.
